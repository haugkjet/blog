### Why web?

#### Pros

- Fast deployments

#### Cons

- Debugging can me more difficult

### Why Free and open source Software

#### Pros

- No licencing costs or changes in licencing
- Huge community for product like Blender, Godot and ThreeJS

#### Cons

- Functionality behind commercial alternatives
- Lack of "procuction pipeline", interop beetween programs
